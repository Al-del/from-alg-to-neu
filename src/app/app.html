About the Event

Where signals meet intelligence

From Algorithm to Neurons is an intensive 4-day event held in Constanta, Romania, bringing together middle and high school students passionate about signal processing and artificial intelligence.

Through hands-on hardware workshops, a timed competitive AI challenge, and a 24-hour hackathon, young participants will explore the bridge between raw digital signals and intelligent systems — no prior experience required.
